About Me

🌟  Hi there! I recently graduated with a bachelor's in Human Centered Design & Engineering from the University of Washington.
🌍 Advocating for accessibility and inclusion is at the core of my design philosophy. I'm dedicated to infusing these principles into every design solution I create.
💡 Beyond the world of design, you'll often find me pursuing diverse interests. I enjoy activities like weightlifting, playing the violin, and watching K-dramas. I also find solace in the pages of a good book and express my creativity through the art of cooking.
🚀 My curiosity extends far beyond design. I have a genuine passion for coding and constantly explore new programming horizons to complement my expertise in design. I'm excited about applying this multidisciplinary skill set to create positive impacts across various industries.
